Contact

Questions, objections, and corrections are all welcome. Everything comes straight to me by email.

Email me directly

There is no contact form on this site on purpose. If you are writing, I would rather have your actual words in my inbox than a form submission stripped of tone and context.

contactunified@proton.me

I read everything myself. Replies are not instant, but they are personal.

What to send

  • A supposed contradiction you think I have not answered well, or at all.
  • A metaphysical argument you want to put to me directly, for or against classical theism.
  • A correction. If I have gotten a source, a translation, or a fact wrong, I want to know.
  • A general question about Orthodox belief or practice, if you are genuinely curious rather than testing me.

Prefer to argue it out live

The Discord server is where the debate happens in real time. Contradictions, metaphysics, and everything in between, with people who actually want to think it through.
