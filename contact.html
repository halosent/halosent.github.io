Contact

Questions, objections, and corrections are all welcome. Everything comes straight to me by email.

Email me directly

There is no contact form on this site, on purpose. If you are writing, your actual words in my inbox are worth more than a form submission stripped of tone and context.

contactunified@proton.me

I read every message myself. I try to give a genuine, personal reply. Even if it takes a little while.

What to send

  • A supposed contradiction you believe I have not answered well. Or not answered at all.
  • A metaphysical argument you want to put to me directly. For, or against, classical theism.
  • A correction. If I have gotten a source, a translation, or a fact wrong. I want to know.
  • A general question about Orthodox belief or practice. If you are genuinely curious, rather than testing me.

Prefer to argue it out live

The Discord server is where the debate happens in real time. Contradictions, metaphysics, and everything in between. With people who genuinely want to think it through.
