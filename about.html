About Us

Who runs this site, what we focus on, and how to get involved.

Who we are

Live Orthodox is run by a team of Christians. We want to build a community that works for everyone, not just people who already agree with us.

How we treat other beliefs

We support every religion. We do not attack anyone's beliefs on this site. Live Orthodox exists to help us understand Christianity, and Christology specifically, better. It was never meant to tear other traditions down.

What we actually focus on

Apologetics, Metaphysics, Philosophy, Christology, and Religion. That's the full list. We don't discuss politics here. We stay away from topics that turn controversial just for the sake of it. If a subject falls outside those five areas, it's probably not going on this site.

Join the team

We're looking for members, from the Church and from other beliefs, to help run the Discord server, write articles, and work on the site itself. This is volunteer work. Nobody gets paid for it. If you want to apply for Moderator or Writer, don't hold back. We'd genuinely like to hear from you.

From Unified

I founded this site. I'm an Eastern Orthodox Christian, and my goal is a space that feels safe and equal for everyone who shows up. Reach out by email or on Discord, I want to hear your arguments, your questions, and your answers to what's already been written.

One thing I want to be clear about. I'm not forcing religion on anyone here. I'm not trying to pressure you into joining Christianity. Come as you are.

Using this site

You have full permission to promote Live Orthodox wherever you'd like. What you don't have permission to do is claim this site as your own.

Want to help build this

Send a message about Moderator or Writer roles, or just say hello.
