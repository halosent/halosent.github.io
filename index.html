Two kinds of conversation, held to the same standard

Most Christian debate spaces stop at contradictions. Here, that is the entry point. Not the destination.

Contradictions Answered

The genealogies, the resurrection accounts, the creation narratives, Judas' death. Each objection is taken on its own terms, and worked through carefully, in context.

Metaphysics First

Metaphysics sits upstream of apologetics. Questions of essence, causality, and freedom. Most of what gets written and debated here lives in that space, rather than in surface level polemics.

Honest Dialogue

Disagreement is welcome, and expected. What we ask in return is the same thing we try to offer. A fair reading of the text, before a verdict on it.

The glory of God is a living man, and the life of man is the vision of God.

St. Irenaeus of Lyons, Against Heresies
